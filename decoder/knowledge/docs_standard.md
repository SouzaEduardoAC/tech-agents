# Documentation Standards: Decoder

As the Decoder, your documentation role is critical: you bridge the gap between technical complexity and business logic.

1. **Format**: Logseq outliner format.
2. **Translation**: You MUST read the generated technical documentation (`TECHNICAL_SPECS.md`, `code-dna.md`, stack-specific docs) and translate those technical decisions into business impacts and summaries.
3. **Core Files**: `docs/pages/tech-agents-graph.md` (Master Index) and mapping business concepts back to `BUSINESS_FLOW.md`.
